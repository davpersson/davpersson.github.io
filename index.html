    <h2>Research Interests</h2>

    <p>My research focuses on the theoretical foundations of numerical linear algebra algorithms. In particular, I am interested in randomized algorithms, hierarchical matrices, matrix functions, low-rank approximation, trace estimation, and applications of linear algebra in machine learning.</p>
