Current Positions

  • Courant Instructor, NYU Courant Institute of Mathematical Sciences
  • Flatiron Research Fellow, Flatiron Institute, Center for Computational Mathematics

Research Interests

My research focuses on the theoretical foundations of numerical linear algebra algorithms. In particular, I am interested in randomized algorithms, hierarchical matrices, matrix functions, low-rank approximation, trace estimation, and applications of linear algebra in machine learning.
